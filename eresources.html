E Resources

E-Resource is an initiative by the Electrical Engineering Society exclusively for its members to provide them with the required resources for academic purposes at their doorstep. It includes a collection of previous year question papers of the department, e-books, tutorials, project reports and other necessary materials, that a student realises the need of. If you cannot find anything and you feel it needs to be here, let us know by commenting below. Also, you can become a contributor to this collection by e-mailing us (ees.eed@ietlucknow.ac.in) the material you have and you want it to be here.


Note: To visit E-Resource Room, you must be signed in from the E-mail id on IET Lucknow domain, i.e. signed in from your rollnumber@ietlucknow.ac.in email id only. No other person is allowed to visit the resource room.
